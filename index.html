Our Mission

Promote sustainable farming practices and provide social services to the community. We believe everyone can learn to grow fruits and vegetables at home and make a difference together.

Source: Chocolate Earth Foundation site.

What we focus on

  • Hands‑on education: aquaponics, hydroponics, aeroponics, microgreens
  • Affordable access to resources and workshops
  • Community support through social services
  • Fundraisers and merchandise that power our programs
